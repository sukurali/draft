DESIGNED FOR
STRENGTH

Based solidly on applied physical laws and empirical knowledge of the structural performance of different materials and geometries, D'Agostino & Associates has evolved to meet changing needs of society and their clients. We apply the principles of statics and dynamics to calculate stability, strength and rigidity of structures to design and analyse complex structural systems to safely resist loading in different environments and conditions.

As structural engineers we are responsible for making creative and efficient use of funds, structural elements and materials to achieve these goals. In today's sustainable evironment, we as structural engineers now must consider the social, economic and evironmental impact as the true cost of our designs.

Building
Envelope

The building envelopes plays an important role in protecting the building and its contents from the elements and for how it affects total building energy use. Basic building envelope attributes assess insulation values, window to wall ratios, glazing characteristics, shading, orientation of glazing, and window operability. In a sustainable society, the design team looks to optimize synergies between the building envelope system and other considerations such as rain water management, heat island reduction, water usage, energy performance, thermal comfort, interior lighting etc.

Building envelope strategies will allow building owners to improve occupant comfort while minimizing energy and water use.

Energy
Modeling

Buildings consume the majority share of all of the energy produced in the Canada and United States. With construction projects ramping up over the next several years, it's critical that engineers prioritize energy performance in their designs. We perform preliminary simple mass energy modelling for new construction and major renovations to explore how to reduce energy loads in the building and accomplish related sustainable goals. Beginning in pre-design and running throughout the design phases we are constantly looking for synergies across the building systems.

An integrative process is a comprehensive approach to building systems and equipment. Project team members look for synergies among systems and components, mutual advantages that can help acheive high levels of buidling performances, human comfort, and environmental benefits. The process should involve rigourous questioning and coordination and challenge typical project assumptions. Team members collaborate to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of every system.
